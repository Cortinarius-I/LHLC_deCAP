Donate Materials

Classroom and therapy materials are always in high demand. Your in-kind donations directly enrich the learning and therapy experience of our students. Items that make a real difference include:

  • Educational books, activity kits and workbooks
  • Sensory and occupational therapy tools and equipment
  • Art and craft supplies, stationery
  • Puzzles, board games and educational toys
  • Furniture, storage and classroom essentials

Please ensure items are clean and in good, usable condition.

Collaborate With Us

We warmly welcome organisations, institutions and individuals who share our commitment to inclusive education and the well-being of children with special needs. Collaborations we are particularly interested in include:

  • Organising workshops, activities or special events for our students
  • Professional development or training programmes for our staff
  • Joint educational programmes or curriculum support partnerships
  • Fundraising initiatives and community events
  • Corporate social responsibility and volunteering partnerships

Volunteer

Volunteers are an invaluable part of our community. Whether you are a student, a professional, or simply someone who cares — your time and presence makes a genuine difference to our students every day. You can contribute by supporting teachers in the classroom, assisting with therapy sessions, helping organise events, or sharing a skill or hobby with our students.

No specialist background is required — just warmth, patience, and a willingness to show up.
