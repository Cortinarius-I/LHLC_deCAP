As parents of a child with Autism and ADHD, our journey has not always been easy. There were moments of uncertainty, worry, and countless questions about how we could best support Sparsh’s growth and future. Finding Little Hearts Learning Centre has truly been a turning point in our lives.



Since joining the centre, we have seen remarkable progress in Sparsh — not just academically, but emotionally and socially as well. His focus has improved, his communication has become clearer, and most importantly, his confidence has grown beautifully. He is learning to manage his daily routines more independently, and his improvement in Activities of Daily Living (ADL) has made a huge difference in our everyday life at home.



What makes Little Hearts truly special is the understanding, patience, and unconditional support they provide to children with Autism and ADHD. They do not just teach — they nurture, guide, and believe in every child’s potential. The structured methods, individualized attention, and loving environment have helped Sparsh feel safe, accepted, and motivated to learn.



Reshmy Ma’am has been more than just a principal ; she has been a mentor and a constant source of encouragement for both Sparsh and us as parents. Her dedication, compassion, and deep understanding of special children give us strength and hope. The entire Little Hearts team works with sincerity, professionalism, and genuine care.



Today, when we see Sparsh smiling, trying new things, and making steady progress, our hearts are filled with gratitude. We are truly thankful to Little Hearts Learning Centre for walking this journey with us and for making such a meaningful difference in our child’s life.
