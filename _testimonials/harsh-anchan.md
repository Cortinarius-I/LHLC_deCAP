Little Hearts Learning Centre is not just a school, but an institution with a holistic approach to help students to be independent. This school has helped my son Harsh Anchan to be well-behaved and helped him to discover his artistic side. Apart from academics this school celebrates many festivals and even conducts activities like cooking, sports, fancy dress which encourages students to not only learn but teaches team-bonding. Harsh started to write with Jumbo chalk but now he is giving Pratham exam. All this has been possible due to the support and motivation of the teacher and school management.
